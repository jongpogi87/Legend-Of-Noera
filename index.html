BETWEEN TWO WORLDS

Some callings begin long before we hear them.

Long ago, an Earth-born Steward came to Zionara and walked among its ancient halls. His search began with hope, passed through loss, and became a longing that would one day threaten the truths he once served.

Now, on Earth, Lady Zion—known as Lady Z—dreams of a world she cannot quite remember. Strange places appear in her drawings. A golden leaf rests where no leaf should be.

Beyond the Veil, the Auret is calling her home.
