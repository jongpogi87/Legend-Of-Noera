EPISODE III

Two Lives

“One heart. Two worlds.”

By day, Lady Zion is an ordinary student on Earth. By night, she walks the paths of Zionara.

As time flows differently between the Twin Worlds, she slowly learns that each life is shaping the other in ways she cannot yet understand.

The more she grows in one world... the more she changes in the other.
